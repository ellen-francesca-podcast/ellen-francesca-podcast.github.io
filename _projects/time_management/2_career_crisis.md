**Description:** Apparently a PhD comes with three crisis: Material crisis, Relevance crisis and the finish crisis. The finish crisis,  or career crisis, is caused by the daunting question of what comes next. To avoid, or more realistically: soften, this crisis, it helps to start exploring alternatives early. In this episode, we discuss our current career ideas about academia, industry, and entrepreneurship and honestly weigh up the pros and cons of each.

*This episode was originally recorded in beginning of 2025. Some political references may be outdated.*
